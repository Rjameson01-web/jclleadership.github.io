Commercial Drift Insights

A diagnostic library on how and why commercial performance drifts — and what it takes to restore it.

What causes pricing to become situational?

Pricing becomes situational when governance is weak, floors are unclear, and decision rights are undefined.

Why do forecasts drift from reality?

Forecasts drift when they are built on optimism rather than pipeline fact, and when they are disconnected from operational capacity.

What causes ownership to blur?

Ownership blurs when roles and mandates are implied rather than explicit, especially under growth pressure.

How does decision-rights ambiguity erode margin?

Ambiguity slows decisions or pushes them to the wrong level, eroding margin through delay or concessions.

Why do sales and operations diverge?

Divergence occurs when each function optimizes locally without a shared operating model or unified plan.
